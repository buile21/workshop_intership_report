> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment** My overriding impression throughout the internship is the dynamic, youthful, and extremely comfortable environment that it brings. The working atmosphere is neither rigid nor heavily pressured; on the contrary, it is always full of positive energy. Everyone supports each other very naturally, and there is almost no gap between interns and senior colleagues.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin** I was fortunate to be guided by dedicated mentors. My mentor never just micro-managed or handed out ready-made answers, but always provided direction and thought-provoking questions so that I could explore, figure things out on my own, and draw my own lessons—the feeling of fixing a bug or optimizing a system by myself was incredibly rewarding. The admin team was extremely thoughtful, handling all procedures quickly from A to Z so that we could fully focus on learning and working.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major** The foundational knowledge from university was put into hands-on practice here in the clearest way. Being directly exposed to cloud architecture, deploying services hands-on, and handling real-world data problems helped me realize so many things. This is the perfect stepping stone to bridge the gap between textbook theory and practical work.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities** This internship felt like an "acceleration phase" for myself; everything was new and worth learning. Besides enhancing logical thinking and system optimization skills, I also learned how to manage time, collaborate smoothly in a team, and present ideas coherently. The practical experience shared by senior colleagues gave me a realistic perspective and a much clearer direction for my future career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit** The culture strongly embodies the "Work hard, play hard" spirit. During the sprint phases for problems or projects, the entire team strove together, shared the pressure, and no one was left behind. The respect for each individual's unique personality while maintaining strong team spirit made me feel like an authentic part of the collective, rather than just a trainee passing through.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits** The flexibility in working hours also helped me well balance university assignments and company tasks. In particular, internal knowledge-sharing sessions are extremely valuable "privileges" that I truly cherish.


### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
  => Being able to completely engage in hands-on practice with cutting-edge technologies, and being trusted with the autonomy to build and operate solutions from the very first steps under dedicated guidance, instead of just doing trivial tasks.

- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
  => Organizing more "edutainment" gameshows (combining play and learning) between different groups so that interns have more opportunities to cross-learn knowledge from one another.

- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
  => Absolutely yes! This is the perfect environment for anyone who wants to step out of their comfort zone, experience real learning and real work, and meet extremely friendly people.


### Suggestions & Expectations
- Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?  
  => I hope the program will continue to be maintained and scaled up, creating more open discussion spaces so that future cohorts can confidently showcase their bold ideas.

- Would you like to continue this program in the future?  
  => I sincerely hope to have the opportunity to continue accompanying the company in new roles to contribute more value to upcoming projects.

- Any other comments (free sharing):  
  => Sending my most sincere thanks to the organizers of the First Cloud Journey program, the mentors, and the admins for creating an incredibly meaningful journey—one full of challenges but extremely fun and memorable in my student life!
