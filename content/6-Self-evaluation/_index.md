### Areas for Improvement

* **Tightening personal discipline:** Need to be stricter with myself in adhering to general rules and managing working hours, ensuring an absolutely professional demeanor in any organizational environment.
* **Sharpening the "problem-solving" mindset:** Avoid rushing to code or fix errors immediately upon spotting a bug. Instead, build the habit of taking a step back to analyze the root cause of the problem deeply and evaluate it from multiple angles before finalizing a solution.
* **Upgrading communication skills:** Be more open and proactive in daily conversations to connect with everyone; meanwhile, practice presenting technical ideas confidently and concisely to handle situations more flexibly at work.
